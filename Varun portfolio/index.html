About Me

Hi, I'm Varun Rathod, a graphic designer passionate about turning ideas into impactful visuals. With a strong eye for design and a deep understanding of aesthetics, I specialize in logo design, posters, flyers, and video editing, creating compelling visuals that capture attention and communicate effectively.
